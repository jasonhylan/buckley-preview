Our Heritage

A Family Tradition of Advocacy

Buckley & Buckley is more than a law firm — it is a family commitment to the Sedalia community. Founded on the principle that every client deserves personal, attentive counsel, our practice has served Pettis County and central Missouri for over three decades.

As a father-son team, James T. Buckley and James P. Buckley combine the depth of seasoned experience with fresh, modern perspective. This partnership ensures that our clients receive counsel that is both time-tested and forward-thinking.

We believe that the best legal representation starts with listening — understanding your unique circumstances, concerns, and goals before charting a path forward. When you work with Buckley & Buckley, you work with attorneys who know your name, return your calls, and fight for your interests as if they were their own.
